Ayubowan! I'm Mahesh

I am a government-licensed Tourist Guide with a deep passion for my island home. For me, tourism isn't just a job; it's about bridging cultures and creating unforgettable memories.


I specialize in "authentic" travel. While we will certainly see the famous icons like Sigiriya and Yala, I love taking my guests off the beaten path—to that secret village spot for the best rice and curry, or a hidden viewpoint in Ella that only the locals know.


Why Travel With Me?

Sri Lanka can be chaotic to navigate alone. With me, you get more than a driver; you get a storyteller, a translator, and a friend who ensures your safety and comfort 24/7. Whether you want a deep-dive into the history of Galle Fort, a surfing adventure in Arugam Bay, or a relaxed wildlife safari, I tailor the pace to suit you.
