Ayubowan! I'm Mahesh

Born and raised here, Sri Lanka isn't something I learned from a textbook — it's just home. I got my tourism license years ago, but honestly what keeps me doing this is the look on people's faces when they taste their first proper rice and curry, or see an elephant up close for the first time.


Yes, we'll go to Sigiriya and Yala — you'd never forgive me if we skipped them. But I'll also take you to the little spots I grew up around. A roadside place in the hill country where the dhal is unreal. A viewpoint above Ella that doesn't show up on any travel blog. That kind of thing.


Why Travel With Me?

Sri Lanka is wonderful but it can be a lot — the traffic, the roads, the heat, not knowing who to trust or where to eat. Having someone local with you just makes the whole thing easier and more fun. I handle the logistics so you don't have to stress, and I'll tell you stories about the places we visit that Google definitely doesn't have. Whether you want to go slow and soak it all in, or pack in as much as possible, we'll figure out a pace that works for you.
